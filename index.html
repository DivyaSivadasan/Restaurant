About Us

WELCOME TO
BURGER LOUNGE

Backed with a solid recipe to satisfy your palate with the most authentic platters, robust flavors, premium ingredients, and loads of creative meals “Burger Lounge” was born. It is the dream child of Mr. Shuhaib Mathilakath Hameed Mon - the founder and mastermind behind Burger Lounge. This global brand is every foodie's haven of meat-based delights, and the most highlighted factor is the brand's farm fresh, succulent, freshly prepared juicy burgers. With properly trained chefs, customer service teams, innovative recipes, and affordable meals, Burger Lounge has become an instant hit among both local and international food lovers. Burger Lounge mushroomed from one outlet and today has over 30+ franchise outlets across Kerala. Plus, to date, we have served over more than 2 million burgers and our sales graphs are always on the rise. As the best food franchise in Kerala, Burger Lounge believes in giving back to the community that has supported us throughout our journey. Are you looking for the best burger shop near? Look no further than Burger Lounge, your neighborhood's best burger store! Burger Lounge has earned its reputation as the best fast food franchise by prioritizing quality, sustainability, and customer satisfaction.
